About

Everything you need to know about AnAutisticDrew

Hey people of the world, I'm Andrew. I'm an Autistic aspiring author, and I thought I could use my combined passions of writing and learning more about something so integral to who I am, to help out other people like me. 

See something I noticed a really long time ago, but never really had the confidence to do anything about, was the fact that the only "resources" for people with Autism weren't actually for people with autism. In worse cases (and the most commonly cited one to date) these "resources" were actually run by people who didn't even believe people with autism could function independently enough to use their services. 

So here we are. At my blog. By an Autistic for Autistics. 

I swear I can't find anything anywhere about how to cope in a work environment, or how to get a diagnosis after age 10. Allistics never think it matters when it does.

This website is a compilation of my autistic anecdotes, how to's, resources, and anything else I can find. 

I hope this site can become a place people can look to for accurate information and help when they need it.
